Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the EPP programme, commission, policies, and bookings

What is the EPP 90/10 commission model?▼
You (the EPP partner) keep 90% of the net margin on every booking. Within this, the actual split with ORN Vacations starts at 80/20 from your verified Sales Base, improving to 75/25 when you hit 1× growth, and 70/30 at 2× growth. This means the more you grow, the larger your share.
How is net margin calculated?▼
Net margin = Gross selling price minus ORN's cost price (accommodation, flights, transfers, activities). GST, TCS, and bank charges are excluded. The final net margin figure as calculated by ORN is binding per your EPP Agreement Clause 1.
When does my commission get paid?▼
Commission statements are raised on the 1st of each month for completed bookings in the prior month. Payment is made by the 15th or last working day of the same month. TDS at 194H (typically 5%) is deducted before payment.
How does my credit facility work?▼
Your credit limit is a multiple of your security deposit — typically 2× to 4×. If you deposited ₹1,00,000 and have a 4× multiplier, your credit limit is ₹4,00,000. You can use this credit to confirm bookings before receiving full client payments. The balance must be remitted by the due date to avoid credit default.
What happens if I miss a balance payment deadline?▼
A Credit Default Event is triggered per Clause 7 of the EPP Agreement. ORN may suspend your platform access, cancel pending bookings, and apply interest at 1.5% per month on the overdue amount. Repeated defaults can lead to agreement termination.
Can I work with sub-agents?▼
No. Policy 06 strictly prohibits EPP partners from working with sub-agents or white-labelling TripFactory products. All bookings must be your own clients. Breach of this policy can result in immediate termination and clawback of commissions.
How do I protect a lead/commission?▼
Log the lead in your assigned CRM within 12 hours of the enquiry. Leads not logged within this window may not be protected if another EPP books the same client. Always quote using TripFactory's official quoting tool to ensure margin protection.
What training is mandatory?▼
All EPP partners must complete: (1) 3-Day Mandatory Induction, (2) Product Overview, (3) Compliance & DPDPA module. Annual refreshers including the DPDPA 2023 update are also mandatory. Non-completion results in platform suspension per Policy 08.
How do I raise a support ticket?▼
Use the Support section in this portal. Select the booking reference, choose the issue category (booking issue, commission query, credit query, technical), and describe the problem. SLA is 2 business hours for urgent matters and 1 business day for standard queries.
What is the exclusivity arrangement?▼
Your EPP grants you a defined territory. Within that territory, ORN will not appoint another EPP partner for the same product lines. However, exclusivity is conditional on meeting minimum sales targets (see Schedule C). Failure to meet targets may result in territory review.
Can I cancel a booking?▼
Yes, subject to the cancellation policy of the specific package and supplier. TripFactory's standard cancellation matrix applies. You remain liable for any cancellation charges. Your commission on cancelled bookings is forfeited unless otherwise agreed in writing.
